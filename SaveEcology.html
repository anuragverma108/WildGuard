Why Saving Ecology Matters

Ecology is the foundation of life on Earth. It's the relationship between living organisms and their environment. Our ecosystems are under threat due to pollution, deforestation, climate change, and loss of biodiversity. Saving our ecology means ensuring the survival of future generations.
